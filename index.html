About CyberPunk: Edgerunners

Immerse yourself in the neon‑lit future

Cyberpunk: Edgerunners is a standalone anime series set in the same universe as Cyberpunk 2077. It follows a street kid named David as he fights to survive in a world of cybernetic enhancements, corporate corruption, and ruthless mercenaries. Watch it for its breathtaking animation, emotional depth, and a story that captures the essence of the cyberpunk genre – rebellion, identity, and the cost of being human.

Why watch it?

If you love intense action, stunning visuals from Studio Trigger, and a heartbreakingly good story, Edgerunners delivers. It’s a must‑watch for any anime fan – even if you’ve never played the game.
